Evictions in Bushwick (2017-2023)

This map shows the effect of the COVID-19 pandemic and New York's consequent eviction moratorium on the Brooklyn neighborhood of Bushwick.

Tenants who experienced financial hardship due to COVID-19 were protected from eviction from March 7, 2020 through January 15, 2022.

Each resdiential eviction carried out by a City Marshall is represented by a dot. Move the slider below to see year-to-year trends.
